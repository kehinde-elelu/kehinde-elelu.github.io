I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computing at Clemson University being advised by Long Cheng, Ph.D.

My current research is focused on using deep learning to automatically detect and mitigate Cyberbullying in online social networking platforms. My research interests are in Machine Learning and Security.

I have a Master's degree in Computer Science from Clemson University and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology.

I will be returning to Apple next summer(2022) in the Siri Home Automation team as an AI/ML Software Engineer Intern.
