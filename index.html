Welcome!

I currently working as PostDoc at Michigan State University in the DeepTech lab where I am currently working on leading the development and commercialization of a smartphone-based acoustic diagnostic system paving the way for product launch

Prior to that, I completed my Ph.D. in Engineering at Clemson University. My dissertation focused on focuses on Human-AI interaction and its application in the construction industry. More specifically on sound equipment and the ability to process this massive audio data using Machine Learning to innovate computerized technologies in managing construction projects.

I also have a bachelor's degree in Engineering from University of Ilorin, Kwara, Nigeria.
